Wario



  1. First Keep You Laptop Straight
  2. Move Yourself Approximately 3-4 Away From Your Laptop
  3. Move Your Wrist In an Accending And Decending Order, A Red Dot Should Appear On Your Right Wrist
  4. Now Press The Play Button, And Again Move Yourself Approximately 3-4 Away From Your Laptop
  5. The Red Paddel Is Your Paddel, That Will Move As Per The Movement Of Your Right Wrist
